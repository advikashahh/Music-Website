MUSIC THERAPIES

Music can heal the wounds,that medicine cannot touch

Compositional music therapy.

Compositional music therapy is both a music therapy intervention and process in which the client and therapist work together to generate an original, permanent musical model.

Improvisation music therapy.

It is process where the client and therapist relate to each other. The client makes up music, musical improvisation, while singing or playing, creating a melody, rhythm, song, or instrumental piece.

Receptive music
therapy.

Receptive:The music used may be live, or a recording. Some types of receptive approaches may include music-assisted relaxation, as well as the use of music and imagery.
